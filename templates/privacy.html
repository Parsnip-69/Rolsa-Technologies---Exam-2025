Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Wednesday 23rd April 2025

At Rolsa Technologies and its affiliates ("we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you interact with our website and services.

  1. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following personal information from you:

    • Full Name / Business Name
    • Email Address
    • Password
    • Date of Birth
    • Residential or Business Address
    • Saved calculations (e.g., energy usage, carbon footprint emissions)

  2. How We Collect Information

    We collect information directly through the following methods:

    • User registration and login forms
    • Booking and scheduling interactions
    • Custom user entries for calculations and reports

    No information is collected automatically.


  3. How We Use Your Information

    Your information is used internally for purposes such as:

    • Managing bookings and appointments
    • Staff and freelancer scheduling
    • Storing and retrieving your saved calculation data
    • Account verification and maintenance

  4. Data Storage

    Your data is stored securely in a SQLite database hosted on our servers. We implement security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.


  5. Data Sharing

    We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or marketing platforms.

    We may share your data with the following, when necessary:

    • Authorised partners and affiliates
    • Freelancers and contractors working directly on service delivery
    • Legal or regulatory authorities, only if required by law

  6. User Rights

    You may have rights depending on your jurisdiction, including the right to:

    • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
    • Withdraw consent (if applicable)
    • Object to or limit data processing
    • Request a copy of the data we hold about you

    To exercise your rights, contact us at: data@rolsa.org.uk

  7. Data Retention

    We retain personal data only as long as necessary for business purposes or to comply with legal obligations.

  8. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant updates will be reflected by a new effective date at the top or communicated directly via email.

  9. Contact Us

    If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at:
