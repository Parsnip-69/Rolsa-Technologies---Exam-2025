Carbon Footprint


Carbon emissions refer to the release of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases into the atmosphere, primarily from human activities like burning fossil fuels. Families contribute through energy use, transportation, and waste, while businesses add emissions from manufacturing, operations, and supply chains. Reducing these footprints is crucial to mitigate climate change and promote sustainability.
